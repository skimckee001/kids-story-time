1. Introduction

Welcome to KidsStoryTime.ai ("we," "our," or "us"). We understand that privacy is paramount, especially when it comes to children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

By using KidsStoryTime.ai, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you disagree with any part of this privacy policy, please do not use our services.

2. COPPA Compliance

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and are committed to protecting the privacy of children under 13 years of age.

KidsStoryTime.ai is designed for use by parents and guardians. Children under 13 should not create accounts or use our services without parental supervision and consent.

Parental Consent

  • We require parental consent before collecting any personal information from children
  • Parents have full control over their children's profiles and data
  • Parents can review, modify, or delete their children's information at any time
  • We do not require children to provide more information than necessary

3. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, password, and subscription details
  • Child Profiles: First name, age, interests (no last names or identifying information)
  • Story Preferences: Themes, characters, and story settings you select
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we don't store credit card numbers)

Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: Stories created, features used, time spent in app
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication and preferences
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage patterns to improve our service

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Social Security numbers
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Biometric data
  • Health or medical information
  • Third-party social media data

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmissions use SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • Access Controls: Employee access is limited and monitored
  • Regular Audits: Security protocols are regularly reviewed and updated
  • Password Protection: User passwords are hashed and salted
  • PCI Compliance: Payment processing meets PCI DSS standards

We NEVER Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share information only in these specific circumstances:

All third-party service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only as directed by us.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You Have the Right To:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Port: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Restrict: Limit how we process your information
  • Object: Opt-out of certain uses (like marketing)

How to Exercise Your Rights

You can manage your data through your account settings or contact us at privacy@kidsstorytime.ai

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through account settings. Upon deletion:

  • Your personal information will be permanently deleted
  • Story history and achievements will be removed
  • Active subscriptions will be cancelled
  • Some anonymized data may be retained for legal/analytical purposes
