The Story Behind KidsStoryTime

A Dad's Journey to Better Bedtimes

Hi, I'm Brett, founder of KidsStoryTime.ai and a dad who knows the nightly struggle all too well.

Like many parents, I found myself exhausted at the end of each day, facing the daunting task of bedtime. My kids would resist, negotiate, and find every excuse to stay up "just five more minutes." Traditional books weren't holding their attention, and I was running out of energy to make up new stories every night.

That's when I realized: what if technology could help create magical, personalized stories where my children were the heroes? Stories that would make them excited for bedtime instead of fighting it?

After months of development and testing with families just like ours, KidsStoryTime.ai was born. Today, we're helping thousands of families transform their bedtime routines into cherished moments of connection and wonder.
