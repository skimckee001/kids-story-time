Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about KidsStoryTime

How does personalization work? +

You enter your child's name, age, and interests. Our AI then creates a unique story where your child is the main character, facing age-appropriate challenges and adventures tailored to their preferences.

Is the content age-appropriate? +

Absolutely! Stories are generated based on your child's age with appropriate vocabulary, themes, and complexity. All content is filtered for safety and educational value.

Can multiple children use one account? +

Yes! Depending on your plan, you can create multiple child profiles. The Family plan supports up to 5 children, each with their own personalized stories and progress tracking.

How do rewards work? +

Children earn stars for reading stories, completing milestones, and maintaining reading streaks. Stars can be collected for badges and achievements, motivating continued reading.

Is there a free trial? +

Yes! All paid plans come with a 30-day free trial. Plus, our free Reader plan lets you create 3 stories per day forever with no credit card required.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Of course! You can cancel your subscription anytime with no questions asked. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
