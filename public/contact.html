Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings or by contacting support. No questions asked!

Absolutely! We're COPPA compliant, use bank-level encryption, and never sell your data.

Can I get a refund?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not completely satisfied.

How do AI illustrations work?

Our AI creates unique, safe images based on your story. The number depends on your subscription plan.

Can I use stories offline?

Yes! You can download stories as PDFs and our app works offline once stories are saved.

Yes! Contact us at business@kidsstorytime.ai for educational pricing and bulk licenses.
